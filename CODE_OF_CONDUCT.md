# Code of Conduct

Codebate welcomes respectful, constructive participation.

## Expected behavior

- Be considerate, specific, and professional.
- Critique ideas and code, not people.
- Respect different backgrounds, experience levels, and points of view.
- Protect private information and report vulnerabilities through `SECURITY.md`.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, threats, discrimination, sexualized conduct, deliberate intimidation, doxxing, and sustained disruption are not tolerated in project spaces.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to `mohamedadelfouda@gmail.com`. Include relevant links or screenshots when possible. The maintainer may edit or remove content, restrict participation, or ban contributors when necessary to protect the community. Reports will be handled as confidentially as practical.

This policy applies in repository discussions, issues, pull requests, and other spaces where someone represents the project.
